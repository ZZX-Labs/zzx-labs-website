Docs

Welcome to the documentation hub of ZZX-Labs, an independent R&D lab focused on software, hardware, Bitcoin infrastructure, cybersecurity, and AI. This section provides all foundational documents, manuals, guides, and records supporting our open-source tools, internal operations, and technical architecture.

These documents are continuously updated and published to ensure transparency, reproducibility, and long-term archival of our methods, models, designs, and development philosophy.

These Documents Contain All Relevant Info

The files below serve as the definitive source for how ZZX-Labs operates: including licensing terms, project overviews, operational manuals, usage guides, and research references. They are intended for contributors, researchers, developers, and anyone auditing our work.
