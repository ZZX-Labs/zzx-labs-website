Thanks

Technology is rarely the work of a single individual.

Every line of code, every protocol, every operating system, every research paper, every hardware design, every open-source project, and every idea builds upon the work of countless others.

This page exists simply to say:

Thank You.

Open Source

To Open-Source Maintainers

Thank you to the maintainers who spend nights, weekends, holidays, and years of their lives fixing bugs, reviewing pull requests, writing documentation, answering questions, and maintaining infrastructure most people never even realize exists.

The modern world runs because of your work.

Bitcoin

To The Bitcoin Community

Thank you to the developers, node operators, miners, educators, Lightning builders, hardware wallet developers, researchers, authors, podcasters, and advocates who continue building the Bitcoin ecosystem.

Bitcoin demonstrated that decentralized digital scarcity was possible and changed the trajectory of technology forever.

Cypherpunks

To The Cypherpunks

Thank you to the people who believed privacy, encryption, and individual liberty mattered long before those ideas became mainstream.

Many of the freedoms modern users take for granted exist because of your persistence.

Research

To Scientists & Researchers

Thank you to the mathematicians, physicists, computer scientists, engineers, chemists, linguists, economists, and researchers whose discoveries form the foundations upon which modern civilization operates.

Most breakthroughs are built upon generations of prior work.

Education

To Teachers

Thank you to teachers, professors, mentors, tutors, librarians, lecturers, and instructors everywhere.

Every engineer, scientist, artist, researcher, and developer began as a student.

Art

To Artists

Thank you to the painters, illustrators, musicians, writers, sculptors, filmmakers, designers, photographers, animators, and creators who remind us that technology alone is not enough.

Civilization needs beauty as much as it needs utility.

Security

To Security Researchers

Thank you to those who identify vulnerabilities, disclose issues responsibly, improve software security, publish research, challenge assumptions, and help make systems safer.

Communities

To Communities Large & Small

Thank you to local hackerspaces, makerspaces, repair communities, Linux user groups, Bitcoin meetups, cryptography groups, amateur radio operators, independent researchers, students, hobbyists, and tinkerers.

Many important ideas begin in small communities.

Infrastructure

To The Invisible Builders

Thank you to network engineers, sysadmins, data center technicians, infrastructure operators, maintainers, database administrators, hardware engineers, and operations teams.

Much of technology works because someone quietly keeps it working.

Future

To Future Contributors

Thank you to the people who have not yet contributed.

The next major discovery, the next important protocol, the next breakthrough tool, the next transformative idea, may come from someone reading this page today.
