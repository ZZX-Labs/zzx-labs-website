Credits & Acknowledgements

No meaningful technology is created in isolation.

ZZX-Labs R&D exists because of thousands of engineers, researchers, mathematicians, artists, hackers, educators, maintainers, scientists, authors, communities, institutions, and open-source contributors whose work made our own work possible.

This page serves as a living acknowledgement of those influences, technologies, projects, organizations, and individuals.

Their writings, software, and activism continue to influence ZZX-Labs research and development.
