Plain Language

The Practical Agreement

By using the ZZX-Labs R&D website, you agree to use it lawfully, respect the infrastructure, verify important information, protect your own systems, avoid abusing public resources, and understand that this site contains experimental research and development materials.

ZZX-Labs agrees to make a good-faith effort to publish useful information, maintain reasonable security practices, document important notices, protect user privacy where possible, and improve the site as the project matures.

1

You Agree To Use The Site Lawfully

You agree not to use ZZX-Labs R&D systems for illegal activity, malicious activity, spam, harassment, credential theft, scraping abuse, malware distribution, unauthorized access, denial-of-service activity, fraudulent payments, impersonation, or other harmful conduct.

2

You Understand The Site Is Under Development

This website is actively changing. Some pages are finished, some are drafts, some are placeholders, some are experimental, and some exist to reserve future structure.

You agree not to treat every unfinished page as a final promise, finished product, final policy, production service, or permanent site structure.

3

You Are Responsible For Your Own Risk

You are responsible for your own devices, wallets, keys, downloads, security decisions, use of software, use of research, reliance on information, and compliance with laws that apply to you.

ZZX-Labs R&D publishes experimental materials. Experimental means “verify before relying.”

4

You Verify Bitcoin Payments Before Sending Funds

You agree to verify Bitcoin addresses, invoices, QR codes, signatures, payment instructions, fees, and amounts before sending funds. Bitcoin transactions are generally irreversible.

If something looks suspicious, stop and verify through official contact channels first.

5

You Report Security Issues Responsibly

If you find a vulnerability, exposed data, fraudulent address, suspicious release, key issue, or phishing attempt, report it privately and responsibly through the proper channels.

Do not exploit systems, exfiltrate data, publish sensitive details prematurely, or harm users.

6

You Understand The Real Policies Still Matter

Satirical Easter Egg

The Absurd Terms Nobody Should Agree To

The following section is a joke. It is intentionally ridiculous and is not intended to replace the real legal documents. It exists because almost nobody reads terms and conditions carefully, which is a civilizational design failure wrapped in a checkbox.

  • You agree to personally defend the website from imaginary goblins using only a rubber duck and a strongly worded README.
  • You agree to name your first production server “Gary” unless the server objects in writing.
  • You agree that any typo found after midnight becomes legally classified as a “linguistic raccoon.”
  • You agree not to summon eldritch compliance auditors without first filing Form CTHULHU-404.
  • You agree that if you click “I agree” without reading anything, a tiny imaginary lawyer may sigh in the distance.
  • You agree to acknowledge that no reasonable person should need a 90-page ritual scroll to use a website.
  • You agree that all ceremonial disputes involving semicolons shall be settled by trial of monospace typography.

See? We do not think anyone will ever read all the actual terms and conditions of the use of this site. If every human did, no one would agree to use any website on the modern internet, intentionally.
