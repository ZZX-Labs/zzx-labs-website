Stickers

The ZZX-Labs Stickers section is intentionally simple: two sticker families, sold in small random packs. One family covers ZZX-Labs logo, branding, slogan, motto, and lab-culture stickers. The other family covers custom art stickers created from ZZX-Labs artwork, project graphics, illustrations, and visual experiments.

Sticker packs are planned in 1-pack, 2-pack, 5-pack, and 10-pack formats. Unless a specific product listing says otherwise, pack contents are randomly selected from the available sticker pool.

Ordering Rules

Random Selection Policy

Random By Default

Sticker packs are randomly assembled from available stock unless the listing offers a specific design selection option.

No Guaranteed Exact Design

Buyers may choose the sticker family and pack size, but exact sticker designs are not guaranteed unless specifically arranged.

Duplicate Handling

Larger packs should avoid duplicates where practical, but duplicates may occur depending on stock, print batch, and available inventory.

Bundle Friendly

Stickers may be included with larger shop orders, project kits, apparel orders, prints, books, or custom client packages.
