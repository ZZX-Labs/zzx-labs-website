Licenses

This page organizes licensing for ZZX-Labs software, source code, firmware, hardware designs, datasets, documentation, publications, templates, graphics, media, commercial products, services, and custom work.

Some releases may be free and open source. Others may require private-use, commercial-use, enterprise, OEM, manufacturing, research-only, or custom written licenses.

Restrictions

Common License Restrictions

Final license terms may vary, but some restrictions are likely to apply unless explicitly waived in writing.

No False Affiliation

Do not imply endorsement, partnership, certification, or sponsorship without written permission.

No Trademark Misuse

ZZX-Labs names, logos, marks, and product identities may require separate permission.

No Secret Extraction

Licenses never grant access to private keys, credentials, infrastructure secrets, or private systems.

No Unsafe Use

Security, firmware, RF, and hardware materials must be used lawfully and safely.

No Unauthorized Redistribution

Paid, private, commercial, or restricted materials may not be redistributed unless permitted.

No Warranty By Default

Materials are generally provided as-is unless a separate support or warranty agreement exists.

Request Workflow

How To Request A License

  1. 01Identify Material:
    Specify the software, code, dataset, template, document, media, hardware, or firmware item.
  2. 02Describe Use:
    Explain personal, research, commercial, internal, enterprise, resale, OEM, or manufacturing use.
  3. 03Define Scope:
    State number of users, projects, devices, clients, deployments, territories, and duration.
  4. 04Review Terms:
    ZZX-Labs reviews license compatibility, risk, attribution, security, and pricing.
  5. 05Issue Invoice:
    If approved, a Bitcoin invoice or payment instruction is issued.
  6. 06Deliver License:
    License text, receipt, product files, access links, or written permission are delivered.
