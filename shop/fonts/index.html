Licensing

Font Licensing Models

Fonts require careful licensing. A font may be free for personal use but restricted for commercial use, web embedding, redistribution, app bundling, logo use, or server-side rendering. Always verify license terms before shipping fonts with a product.

Free

Open or free font packages for public resources, educational materials, and selected projects.

Commercial

Paid use in websites, apps, products, client work, publications, dashboards, and brand systems.

Webfont

Licensing for font files embedded in websites, dashboards, portals, web apps, and static pages.

App Embedding

Licensing for fonts bundled into desktop apps, mobile apps, firmware, or software packages.

Enterprise

Organization-wide font use across teams, documents, products, internal systems, and public releases.

Exclusive

Custom typefaces or custom modifications created for one client, product, or brand.
