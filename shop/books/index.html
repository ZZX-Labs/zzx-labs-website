Books

The ZZX-Labs Books catalog contains long-form publications, technical books, manuals, monographs, field guides, educational texts, research collections, artistic works, strategic studies, and reference editions authored, edited, commissioned, or published by ZZX-Labs R&D and its affiliated projects.

Books may be distributed digitally, physically, openly, commercially, privately, educationally, or under custom licensing arrangements. Future releases may include PDF, EPUB, HTML, Markdown, paperback, hardcover, signed, archival, and collector editions.
