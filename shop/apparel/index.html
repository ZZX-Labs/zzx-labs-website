Apparel

The ZZX-Labs Apparel section is the site merch booth: practical clothing, wearable project art, Bitcoin culture gear, lab shirts, hats, beanies, hoodies, cloaks, patches, and comfortable pants for workstations, workshops, travel, labs, studios, and daily wear.

Apparel should be simple, durable, wearable, and useful. The focus is clothing people will actually wear: shirts, hoodies, full-zips, hats, beanies, patches, comfortable pants, field-friendly layers, and occasional art-driven merch tied to ZZX-Labs projects.

Product Standards

What Apparel Should Optimize For

Comfort

Soft fabrics, good fit, breathable materials, useful cuts, and clothing people will actually wear.

Durability

Good stitching, durable blanks, washable prints, strong embroidery, and long-term wear.

Simple Branding

Clean marks, project names, small logos, back prints, sleeve marks, and wearable designs.

Useful Colors

Black, charcoal, grey, olive, navy, brown, natural cotton, muted earth tones, and low-profile colors.

Real Sizing

Clear size charts, tall sizes where possible, larger sizes where possible, and practical garment measurements.

Low Waste

Avoid overproducing inventory. Favor print-on-demand, small runs, preorders, or stocked basics that move.

Production

Production Models

Print-On-Demand

Best for early merch, testing designs, reducing warehouse waste, and avoiding dead inventory.

Small Batch

Useful for proven designs, event runs, project launches, and controlled inventory.

Embroidered Items

Hats, beanies, patches, hoodies, full-zips, and cleaner professional merch.

Screen Printing

Better for proven shirt designs, larger runs, strong graphics, and band-shirt style apparel.

DTG Printing

Flexible for art-heavy shirts, smaller batches, complex graphics, and print-on-demand workflows.

Cut-and-Sew

Future custom pants, cloaks, unusual garments, and original clothing patterns if demand proves real.

Made-To-Order
