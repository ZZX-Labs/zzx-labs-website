General

General Shop Questions

What is the ZZX-Labs Shop?

The shop is the marketplace gateway for ZZX-Labs software, hardware kits, research products, publications, datasets, creative assets, apparel, services, licenses, and future subscriptions.

Is the shop active?

The shop structure is active, but checkout automation, product listings, inventory, shipping, returns, receipts, and payment workflows are still under development.

Are all products made by ZZX-Labs?

No. Some products may be ZZX-Labs originals. Others may be stocked, curated, bundled, or referenced external brands owned by their respective manufacturers.

Where should I browse first?

Use Categories to browse by product type, Brands to browse by manufacturer or project family, and Featured for curated releases.

Payments

Bitcoin Payment Questions

What payment methods are accepted?

ZZX-Labs shop payments are intended to be Bitcoin-only, using on-chain Bitcoin for larger payments and Lightning Network for smaller or faster payments once deployed.

Do you accept fiat, cards, PayPal, or altcoins?

No. The intended shop model is Bitcoin-only. No credit cards, PayPal, fiat checkout, or altcoins.

Should I send BTC to a static address?

No large or formal shop payment should rely on a copied static address. Final checkout should use a unique invoice, order ID, address, or Lightning invoice.

How many confirmations are required?

Confirmation requirements should vary by item value, payment rail, and delivery risk. High-value or physical orders may require stronger confirmation before fulfillment.

Can I use Lightning Network?

Lightning is planned for small purchases, subscriptions, memberships, API access, and fast checkout, but availability depends on the final payment infrastructure.

Where can I read payment rules?

Use the Shop Payments page for shop checkout rules and the Payments Hub for site-wide Bitcoin payment policy.

Digital Products

Downloads, Software, and Data

When do digital downloads unlock?

Digital products should unlock after payment is detected and the required confirmation threshold is reached. Final rules will depend on the checkout system.

Will files include hashes?

Important downloads should include SHA256 or stronger checksums, and higher-risk releases should include signatures where appropriate.

Can I redownload purchases?

Future account or receipt systems may support redownloads. Until then, preserve receipts, order IDs, and transaction IDs.

Are datasets licensed?

Yes. Dataset rights may vary. Some may be open, some restricted, some commercial, and some research only. Read product-specific license notes.

Physical Goods

Hardware, Kits, Apparel, and Shipping

Do you sell hardware?

The shop is intended to support ZZX-Labs hardware kits, curated third-party hardware, Bitcoin tools, security kits, microcontroller systems, and physical lab products.

Do you stock external brands?

Yes, the shop may stock or curate external brands such as security tools, SDR devices, hardware hacking tools, microcontrollers, and development boards.

When does shipping begin?

Physical goods should enter packing and shipping only after payment reaches the required confirmation threshold and the order passes review.

Are returns allowed?

Return rules will vary by product type. Hardware, kits, apparel, prints, limited editions, and custom goods may each have different return conditions.

Brands

Brand and Vendor Questions

What is an internal brand?

An internal brand is owned, operated, developed, or incubated by ZZX-Labs R&D or the broader ZZX project ecosystem.

What is an external brand?

An external brand is owned by its manufacturer. ZZX-Labs may stock, resell, bundle, review, integrate, or reference it without claiming ownership.

Does listing imply partnership?

No. Brand listings do not imply ownership, endorsement, sponsorship, affiliation, or partnership unless explicitly stated.

How do I report brand concerns?

Contact ZZX-Labs for trademark, attribution, licensing, reseller, manufacturer, or rights-holder concerns.

Support

Order Support

For payment issues, preserve your order ID, invoice ID, transaction ID, timestamp, amount, wallet software, destination address or invoice, and any official checkout page information. Do not send seed phrases, private keys, wallet files, passwords, or sensitive credentials.

Delayed Payment

Provide transaction ID, order ID, amount, timestamp, and payment address or invoice.

Wrong Amount

Underpayments, overpayments, and duplicate payments require manual review and clear records.

Download Problem

Provide product name, order ID, file name, error message, browser, and timestamp.

Shipping Problem

Provide order ID, item, shipping status, tracking if available, and issue description.

Security

Important Security Reminder

Never trust payment addresses from screenshots, reposted messages, unknown mirrors, unofficial chat accounts, or copied QR codes. Always verify official pages, check active alerts, and contact support before large or suspicious payments.
