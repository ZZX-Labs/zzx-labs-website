Rainbow Tables

Rainbow Table Research Sets

Rainbow tables are precomputed hash lookup datasets used in password security research, defensive password auditing, academic study, benchmark testing, and controlled lab education. ZZX-Labs may catalog rainbow table sets and compatible tooling for lawful research and defensive use only.

These resources are not intended for unauthorized access, credential theft, account compromise, intrusion, harassment, extortion, or misuse against systems that the user does not own or have explicit permission to test.

Research Use

Academic study, cryptographic education, password-security research, and controlled demonstrations.

Defensive Auditing

Testing password policies on systems where explicit authorization has been granted.

Benchmarking

Measuring storage, lookup speed, hash coverage, tool performance, and defensive mitigation strategies.

Complete Sets

Future full-set catalogs, segmented volumes, manifests, checksums, and lawful distribution references.

Tooling

Research tooling references, documentation, verification scripts, indexing notes, and lab setup guides.

Mitigation Research

Materials explaining salts, slow hashes, memory-hard KDFs, rate limiting, MFA, and modern password defense.

Safety

Responsible Dataset Use

Dataset users are responsible for lawful use, safe handling, license compliance, privacy protection, security controls, attribution, and avoiding misuse. Sensitive, security-related, or dual-use datasets must be handled only in authorized environments and according to applicable law.

Lawful Use

Use datasets only for permitted, licensed, lawful, ethical, and properly authorized purposes.

Privacy

Respect privacy, minimization, aggregation, retention limits, access controls, and applicable data-protection laws.

Security

Protect datasets from unauthorized access, corruption, leakage, tampering, and misuse.

Dual-Use Review

Security datasets, rainbow tables, malware metadata, and OSINT datasets require extra care and authorization.
