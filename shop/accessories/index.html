Accessories

The ZZX-Labs Accessories section is the practical merch table for small, useful, shippable items: stickers, pins, keychains, lanyards, bags, pouches, mugs, bottles, notebooks, desk mats, mousepads, cable straps, cases, patches, labels, and everyday lab gear.

Accessories should be affordable, durable, useful, and easy to ship. The focus is practical merchandise that supports the ZZX-Labs ecosystem without creating unnecessary warehouse deadweight.

Shipping

Fulfillment Notes

Accessories are generally easier to ship than apparel or hardware, but custom items, bulky desk mats, mugs, bottles, bags, and fragile items may require special packaging, tracking, or replacement review.

Flat Mailers

Useful for stickers, patches, small labels, decals, and lightweight flat accessory orders.

Padded Mailers

Useful for pins, keychains, patches, small pouches, cable gear, and mixed accessory packs.

Boxed Shipping

Required for mugs, bottles, desk mats, bags, larger bundles, and fragile items.

Tracked Shipping

Recommended for most physical accessory orders, especially bundles and custom items.

Returns

Return terms may vary for custom, print-on-demand, damaged, opened, used, or made-to-order items.

International Orders

May require customs review, shipping restrictions, taxes, duties, and destination checks.
