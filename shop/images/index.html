GIF-Warfare Bundles

GIF-Warfare Bundles are joke-branded response GIF packs: “lethally useful” animated reaction libraries for replies, chats, forums, project updates, support threads, group chats, and dramatic internet diplomacy. They are not weapons, not threat material, and not actual warfare tools. They are expressive image packs with an intentionally absurd name.
