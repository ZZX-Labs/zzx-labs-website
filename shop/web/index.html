Request Workflow

How Web Development Work Starts

  1. 01Initial Request:
    Client describes the website, backend, API, template, component, or architecture need.
  2. 02Scope Review:
    ZZX-Labs reviews goals, pages, data, design, security, payment requirements, and deployment target.
  3. 03Proposal:
    A scope, deliverables list, payment structure, timeline, and dependencies are defined.
  4. 04Bitcoin Invoice:
    Payment terms are issued through the Bitcoin payment workflow.
  5. 05Build:
    Frontend, backend, API, templates, documentation, or deployment kits are created.
  6. 06Review:
    Client reviews deliverables, requests corrections, and verifies expected behavior.
  7. 07Delivery:
    Final code, docs, assets, configs, and deployment notes are delivered.
