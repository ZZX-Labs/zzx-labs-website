Safety

Hardware Safety and Compliance

Hardware projects may involve electricity, batteries, radio equipment, heat, sharp tools, soldering, firmware flashing, and mechanical assembly. Users are responsible for safe handling, local regulations, lawful use, and proper supervision.

Electrical Safety

Verify voltage, polarity, current, insulation, grounding, and safe power handling.

Battery Safety

Use proper LiPo charging, protection circuits, storage, and fire-safe handling.

RF Compliance

Wireless and SDR equipment may be regulated; users must obey local laws.

Authorized Use

Security hardware must be used only in lawful, authorized, controlled environments.
