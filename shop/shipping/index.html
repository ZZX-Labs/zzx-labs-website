Shipping

This page defines the intended shipping policy for ZZX-Labs Shop physical products, including hardware kits, electronics, apparel, stickers, prints, accessories, collectibles, limited editions, and future physical product releases.

Shipping begins only after the order is reviewed, Bitcoin payment is confirmed according to product risk, and the item is ready for packing and fulfillment.

Shipping Workflow

Order Handling Process

  1. 01Order Created:
    Customer places an order for a physical shop item.
  2. 02Payment Issued:
    Customer pays the order invoice using Bitcoin on-chain or Lightning where supported.
  3. 03Payment Confirmed:
    Payment reaches the required confirmation threshold for the product type and value.
  4. 04Order Reviewed:
    Order is checked for inventory, restrictions, address validity, payment match, and shipping readiness.
  5. 05Packing Queue:
    Item is packed, labeled, documented, and prepared for carrier pickup or drop-off.
  6. 06Shipment Created:
    Tracking information is generated where supported by the carrier.
  7. 07Order Shipped:
    Shipment is transferred to carrier and marked as shipped.
  8. 08Delivery Follow-Up:
    Delivery issues, damage claims, lost packages, or returns are handled through support.

Payment Confirmation

Shipping Does Not Begin Before Payment Confirmation

Physical products should not enter the shipping queue until Bitcoin payment is confirmed according to the product’s risk and value. High-value hardware, limited editions, custom goods, or restricted products may require additional confirmation or manual review.

Carriers

Shipping Carriers and Methods

Final carrier support is not yet active. Future shipping methods may include USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, regional carriers, insured shipping, tracked shipping, signature delivery, or special handling for fragile electronics.

Tracked Shipping

Preferred for most physical products where tracking is available.

Insured Shipping

Recommended for higher-value electronics, kits, hardware, and limited editions.

Signature Delivery

May be required for high-value, restricted, or sensitive shipments.

Special Handling

Electronics, fragile prints, batteries, or sensitive equipment may require special packaging.

Restrictions

Shipping Restrictions

Some products may not be shippable to all jurisdictions. Restrictions may apply because of export controls, sanctions, carrier rules, battery rules, radio equipment laws, customs requirements, safety rules, or product category restrictions.

Export Controls

Some electronics, security tools, radio devices, or technical kits may require review before export.

Battery Rules

Products containing batteries may require special shipping classification or may be excluded.

Radio Equipment

SDR, RF, antennas, and wireless equipment may be restricted in some jurisdictions.

No-Service Regions

Some regions may be unavailable until legal, compliance, or governmental approval is confirmed.

Packaging

Packing Standards

ZZX-Labs physical shipments should be packed to protect the product, document the order, and reduce damage risk. Electronics should use anti-static packaging where appropriate. Prints should use protective mailers or tubes. Hardware kits should include a manifest or packing list where practical.

Lost / Damaged

Lost, Delayed, or Damaged Packages

If a package is delayed, lost, damaged, incomplete, or misdelivered, preserve all order records, tracking information, photos, packaging, labels, carrier notices, and payment records before contacting support.
