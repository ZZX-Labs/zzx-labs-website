Brand Notice

Internal vs External Brands

ZZX-Labs internal brands are owned, operated, incubated, or developed by ZZX-Labs R&D or its affiliated project ecosystem. These may include software products, research programs, publications, datasets, hardware kits, startup concepts, and future subsidiary product lines.

External brands listed on this page are not owned by ZZX-Labs unless explicitly stated. They may be stocked, resold, bundled, reviewed, referenced, used in kits, integrated into prototypes, or recommended as part of a broader research or development workflow.

External brand names, product names, logos, trademarks, and intellectual property remain the property of their respective owners. Listing an external brand does not imply ownership, endorsement, sponsorship, certification, affiliation, partnership, or authorization unless explicitly stated in writing.

Stocking Status

Brand Availability States

Every brand can eventually have a stocking state so users understand whether a brand is currently stocked, planned, under review, available by request, used in kits, or referenced only for compatibility.

Stocked

Items from the brand are available or intended to be available directly through the shop.

Curated

Items are selected for compatibility, kit inclusion, or recommended workflows.

Bundled

Items may be included inside ZZX-Labs kits, research builds, or hardware bundles.

By Request

Items are not stocked by default but may be sourced for consulting, labs, or custom builds.

Compatibility

Brand appears for compatibility, documentation, tooling, or support references only.

Planned
