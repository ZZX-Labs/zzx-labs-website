Limited Editions

The ZZX-Labs Limited Editions section is for numbered, small-batch, signed, archived, project-specific, artist-specific, and collector-oriented releases that are intentionally produced in restricted quantities.

Limited editions may include prints, books, papers, art objects, apparel, stickers, patches, datasets, physical media, hardware kits, drive archives, signed documents, and special project bundles.

Rules

Edition Rules

Fixed Quantity

Each limited edition should state the planned edition size when known.

Edition Numbers

Numbered items may use formats like 001/100, 002/100, or another documented scheme.

No Automatic Restock

Limited editions should not be treated as always-available items.

Variant Disclosure

Color, size, material, print batch, signature, or packaging variants should be documented where practical.

Condition Notes

Physical items should include condition, packaging, shipping, damage, and replacement rules.

Receipt Records

Bitcoin payment, order reference, shipment record, and edition metadata may be retained for verification.

Verification

Provenance, Hashes, and Manifests

Limited editions should be easier to verify than ordinary shop items. Where appropriate, releases may include manifests, SHA256 hashes, signed records, edition metadata, release notes, print batch records, and provenance references.

Edition Manifest

JSON or PDF record listing edition title, edition size, edition number, release date, and included files or objects.

SHA256 Hashes

Hashes for digital archives, PDFs, datasets, source bundles, image files, audio files, and release packages.

Signed Records

Optional signed manifests, creator signatures, GPG signatures, or verification references.

Certificate Notes

Optional certificate of authenticity, printed insert, archive card, or edition record.

Release Notes

Version, edition date, materials, source files, credits, licensing terms, and production notes.

Archive Copy

Optional archive copy for future verification, reproducibility, restoration, or replacement review.

Payments

Bitcoin-Only Limited Edition Sales

Limited edition sales are intended to be paid in Bitcoin. High-value items may require stronger confirmation, manual review, invoice records, insurance, shipping confirmation, and fraud-risk checks before fulfillment.

On-Chain BTC

Preferred for higher-value limited editions, physical items, and signed releases.

Lightning

Future option for small limited items, sticker packs, patch packs, and lower-value releases.

Invoices

Manual invoice workflow for signed items, custom editions, collector items, and bundled releases.

Receipts

Payment records, transaction IDs, edition records, shipping state, and confirmation notes.

Fulfillment

Shipping and Handling

Limited editions may require careful handling. Prints, books, art, hardware kits, drives, and collector objects may need protective packaging, tracking, insurance, signature confirmation, or manual shipment review.

Flat Shipping

Useful for papers, certificates, art cards, small prints, stickers, patches, and thin documents.

Tube Shipping

Useful for posters, large prints, charts, technical diagrams, and rolled artwork.

Boxed Shipping

Required for books, kits, drives, objects, framed pieces, and mixed bundles.

Insurance

Recommended for signed, rare, high-value, fragile, numbered, or difficult-to-replace items.

Signature Delivery

May be required for high-value items or collector shipments.

International Orders

May require customs review, destination checks, duties, taxes, and shipping-cost confirmation.

Shipping Returns Support

Returns

Limited Edition Return Notes

Return terms may be stricter for limited editions than ordinary items. Signed, numbered, custom, opened, damaged after delivery, digital, made-to-order, or one-of-one items may not be returnable except where required by law or where ZZX-Labs confirms an order or fulfillment error.

Final Sale Risk

Some limited editions may be final sale due to scarcity, customization, signature, or production model.

Damage Review

Shipping damage should be documented immediately with photos, packaging evidence, and carrier details.

Wrong Item

Wrong item, wrong edition number, or missing components require order-record review.

Digital Items

Downloaded digital limited editions may have limited or no return eligibility after access is delivered.
