Collectibles

The ZZX-Labs Collectibles section is an order-on-demand collectible sourcing desk. Instead of holding warehouse inventory, ZZX-Labs may help source statues, figures, models, prop replicas, display pieces, art objects, boxed collectibles, and specialty items from existing sellers.

The customer pays ZZX-Labs in Bitcoin. ZZX-Labs then places the order with the seller or authorized retailer, accounts for fiat conversion, shipping, taxes, service markup, availability, and fulfillment risk, then coordinates delivery where possible.

Model

Order-On-Demand Collectible Sourcing

This category is designed to avoid dead stock. ZZX-Labs does not need to buy large inventory in advance. Products can be sourced from third-party shops, specialty retailers, official stores, auction listings, collectible marketplaces, manufacturer storefronts, or direct-order vendors.

ZZX-Labs is not automatically affiliated with any third-party seller, brand, studio, licensor, manufacturer, or marketplace unless explicitly stated. Third-party trademarks and product names belong to their respective owners.

Customer Request

The buyer requests a collectible, link, brand, character, edition, seller, or general category.

Availability Check

ZZX-Labs checks seller availability, price, shipping, taxes, lead time, restrictions, and reliability.

Bitcoin Quote

The customer receives a BTC quote including item cost, markup, conversion buffer, shipping, and handling.

Order Placement

After BTC payment confirmation, ZZX-Labs places the order with the seller where feasible.

Fulfillment

The seller may ship directly to the buyer, or to ZZX-Labs first if inspection, bundling, or forwarding is needed.

Records

Order reference, BTC transaction, seller invoice, tracking, and fulfillment notes may be recorded.

Seller Sources

Possible Source Channels

Collectibles may be sourced from established retailers, official product pages, manufacturer shops, specialty collectible sites, auction sites, marketplace listings, or direct sellers. Example source types may include collectible retailers similar to Sideshow-style shops, official brand shops, figure retailers, prop shops, hobby stores, and specialty import sellers.

Official Retailers

Manufacturer or licensor-approved storefronts when available.

Collectible Retailers

Specialty collectible shops selling statues, figures, props, and premium display pieces.

Hobby Stores

Model kits, miniatures, display models, paintable figures, and hobby-grade collectibles.

Marketplaces

Marketplace listings where seller reputation, availability, condition, and buyer protection can be checked.

Auctions

Auction purchases may require manual review, maximum bid terms, timing rules, and additional risk acceptance.

Import Sellers

International collectible sellers where customs, shipping cost, delays, and authenticity risks must be reviewed.

Pricing

Bitcoin Quote Structure

Quotes should account for the full cost of acquisition and delivery. The BTC amount may include seller price, sales tax, shipping, import duties, insurance, payment-processing friction, BTC volatility buffer, service markup, and any forwarding or inspection cost.

Item Cost

The listed price from the seller at the time of quote.

Markup

ZZX-Labs sourcing, coordination, BTC conversion, order handling, and service margin.

Shipping

Seller shipping, direct shipping, forwarding, packaging, tracking, insurance, and oversized-item handling.

Tax / Duties

Sales tax, customs duties, import fees, VAT, brokerage, or destination charges where applicable.

BTC Buffer

Volatility buffer for fiat conversion, timing risk, network fees, and quote expiration.

Manual Review

Additional review may be required for high-value, fragile, rare, international, or auction items.

Fulfillment

Shipping and Delivery

Whenever practical, direct-to-customer shipping is preferred. Some items may need to ship to ZZX-Labs first for inspection, consolidation, forwarding, documentation, or special packaging.

Direct Ship

The seller ships directly to the buyer after ZZX-Labs places the order.

Forwarded Ship

The item ships to ZZX-Labs first, then is forwarded to the buyer.

Inspection Option

High-value items may be inspected before forwarding, if arranged and paid for.

Insurance

Fragile, expensive, rare, or oversized collectibles should use insured shipping where available.

Oversized Items

Large statues, framed pieces, display objects, and heavy items may require custom shipping quotes.

International Items

International orders may involve customs, duties, long delays, import restrictions, or seller limitations.

Risk

Availability, Authenticity, and Return Limits

Third-party collectibles can sell out, change price, arrive damaged, be delayed, be canceled by the seller, or differ from listing photos. ZZX-Labs can coordinate the purchase, but seller policies and carrier realities still matter.

Availability

Items are not guaranteed available until the order is accepted by the seller.

Price Changes

Seller prices may change before payment or before order placement.

Condition

Condition depends on seller description, photos, packaging, and marketplace standards.

Authenticity

Authenticity should be checked where possible, but third-party sellers may still carry risk.

Returns

Returns depend on seller terms, item condition, custom status, shipping damage, and buyer timing.

Cancellations

If the seller cancels or stock disappears, the buyer may need refund, replacement, or re-quote options.

Request

What To Send For A Collectible Request

Product Link

Send the seller URL, official product page, marketplace listing, or auction link.

Target Price

State the maximum BTC or fiat-equivalent budget before markup, shipping, taxes, and insurance.

Condition

New, sealed, used, display condition, box condition, graded, complete, or acceptable wear.

Shipping Address Region

Provide country/state/region for shipping estimate without exposing full address until order stage.

Insurance Preference

State whether insured shipping, signature confirmation, or extra packaging is required.

Deadline

State whether this is time-sensitive, a gift, event-related, or flexible.
