Kits

ZZX-Labs Kits are curated bundles for building, learning, researching, prototyping, testing, and deploying technology systems. Kits may include hardware, firmware, software, source code, documentation, datasets, diagrams, templates, tools, accessories, and Bitcoin-native workflows.

Kits may be ZZX-Labs original builds, curated external brand bundles, educational lab kits, research kits, security kits, SDR kits, Bitcoin infrastructure kits, media kits, or custom project kits assembled for a specific use case.

Safety / Compliance

Kit Safety Notes

Kits may involve electricity, batteries, soldering, sharp tools, RF equipment, firmware flashing, network devices, security tools, and experimental hardware. Users are responsible for lawful use, safe handling, local regulations, and proper supervision.

Electrical Safety

Verify voltage, current, polarity, grounding, insulation, battery safety, and proper power handling.

Firmware Safety

Use correct board targets, preserve recovery options, and avoid flashing incompatible firmware.

RF Compliance

SDR and wireless tools must be used under applicable laws and authorized test conditions.

Security Tool Use

Security kits must be used only in lawful, authorized, controlled environments.
