Memberships

ZZX-Labs R&D memberships are high-value annual access tiers reserved for serious professional, institutional, research, policy, security, technical, and mission-aligned clients. Memberships are intended for qualified communities such as intelligence-adjacent research groups, NGOs, governments, defense contractors, DoD, DoJ, law enforcement organizations, think tanks, R&D labs, policy organizations, journalists, scientists, researchers, academia, and private clients with legitimate technical or strategic needs.

Memberships are paid annually in Bitcoin. Tier pricing is planned from approximately $500 USD-equivalent per year up to $50,000 USD-equivalent per year, quoted and settled in BTC at the time of invoice.

Access Review

Membership Requires Review

Membership is not automatic. ZZX-Labs may review the applicant, organization, intended use, jurisdiction, risk profile, mission alignment, payment source, legal constraints, data-handling needs, and security requirements before accepting a membership.

Identity / Organization

Applicants may need to identify their organization, role, professional purpose, and expected use.

Lawful Purpose

Membership must be used for lawful research, defensive work, policy, education, development, journalism, or legitimate private work.

Jurisdiction Review

Some countries, clients, sectors, or requests may require approval, may be restricted, or may be declined.

Scope Review

Higher tiers may require a written scope, access agreement, use restrictions, and support boundaries.

No Misuse

Membership does not authorize illegal access, fraud, harassment, surveillance abuse, credential theft, or harmful activity.

Revocation

Membership may be suspended or terminated for misuse, misrepresentation, nonpayment, legal risk, or policy violation.

Not A Clearance / Not A Credential

Membership Does Not Create Authority

A ZZX-Labs membership is a private access and service relationship. It does not create government authority, law-enforcement authority, security clearance, official endorsement, classified access, procurement status, employment, agency affiliation, or permission to act on behalf of ZZX-Labs or any referenced institution.

No Official Endorsement

Membership does not imply endorsement by any government, agency, NGO, alliance, contractor, university, or institution.

No Classified Access

Membership is not a clearance and does not grant access to classified, restricted, or non-public government systems.

No Representation

Members may not represent themselves as ZZX-Labs agents, employees, partners, or official representatives without written agreement.

No Procurement Guarantee

Membership does not guarantee contracts, procurement status, grants, partnerships, or future work.
