ZZX-Labs Shop

The ZZX-Labs Shop is the Bitcoin-only storefront and order gateway for ZZX-Labs R&D products, services, downloads, publications, datasets, custom work, subscriptions, memberships, physical goods, media assets, software releases, hardware kits, and professional technology work.

This shop is organized as a working catalog for a research and development laboratory rather than a conventional retail store. Some sections will eventually contain automated products. Some sections require manual quote, approval, verification, licensing, access control, or custom fulfillment. Some are placeholders for future product registries. All payment workflows are intended to settle through Bitcoin.
