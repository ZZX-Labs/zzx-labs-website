ZZX-Labs Shop

Welcome to the official ZZX-Labs Shop—your destination for sovereign tools, research kits, software bundles, and exclusive digital content. Every product we offer is designed, built, or curated by the ZZX-Labs collective to empower individuals in open-source development, secure computation, and decentralized research.

All purchases are conducted exclusively in Bitcoin—either on-chain or over the Lightning Network. No fiat, no surveillance, no compromise.

What You Can Find Here

The ZZX-Labs Shop features a variety of high-integrity digital and physical products including:

  • Downloadable Software & Scripts – Tools, utilities, frameworks, or code packages written by our team.
  • Hardware Kits – Raspberry Pi builds, sensor packs, crypto security kits, and open hardware projects.
  • Data – Exclusive documentation and raw data sets supporting our projects.
  • Books, PDFs, & Publications – Published content from ZZX-Labs researchers and collaborators.
  • Apparel & Stickers – For supporters and contributors aligned with our mission.
