ZZX-Labs Shop

The ZZX-Labs Shop is the marketplace gateway for software, hardware, research, publications, datasets, documentation, media, design assets, apparel, services, memberships, licenses, and Bitcoin-native products created or curated by ZZX-Labs R&D.

Purchases are intended to be Bitcoin-only: on-chain for larger settlement and Lightning Network for small, fast, recurring, or API-style payments once fully deployed.

Security

Verify Downloads and Payments

Verify Hashes

Digital downloads should eventually include SHA256 or stronger checksums.

Verify Signatures

Important releases should be signed and checked against the active key history.

Verify Addresses

Always verify Bitcoin addresses, invoices, QR codes, amounts, and expiry before sending funds.

Watch Alerts

Check active alerts for phishing, fake shop pages, fraudulent payment addresses, or compromised releases.
