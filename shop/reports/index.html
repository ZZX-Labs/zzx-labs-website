Integrity

Report Integrity

Reports should distinguish observed facts from interpretation, assumptions, estimates, and projections. Where possible, reports should cite sources, preserve methodology, identify limitations, describe evidence, and state confidence levels.

Time-sensitive reports may become stale. Technical reports may be affected by software releases, protocol changes, new vulnerabilities, legal changes, market events, or updated datasets.
