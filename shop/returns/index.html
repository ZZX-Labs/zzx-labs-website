Returns

This page defines the intended return and exchange policy for ZZX-Labs Shop physical products, including hardware kits, electronics, apparel, stickers, prints, accessories, collectibles, limited editions, and future physical goods.

Digital downloads, software, datasets, licenses, subscriptions, custom work, and services may follow different refund or cancellation rules because they cannot always be physically returned.

Scope

What This Page Covers

Physical Goods

Hardware kits, electronics, apparel, stickers, prints, accessories, collectibles, and physical merchandise.

Shipping Damage

Items damaged in transit, incomplete packages, crushed packaging, missing parts, or carrier-related issues.

Wrong Items

Incorrect item, wrong quantity, wrong variant, mistaken shipment, or mismatched order contents.

Defective Items

Dead-on-arrival electronics, failed components, missing kit parts, or defective manufacturing conditions.

Not Always Returnable

Items That May Have Special Rules

Some product categories may not be eligible for standard returns once delivered, downloaded, opened, assembled, activated, customized, used, flashed, programmed, damaged, modified, or consumed.

Digital Downloads

Software, PDFs, datasets, media, templates, and source bundles may be non-returnable after delivery.

Licenses

Commercial-use rights, private licenses, subscriptions, and memberships may require separate cancellation rules.

Custom Work

Custom research, software, hardware, design, and documentation may be governed by separate written terms.

Opened Electronics

Electronics may be restricted after opening, soldering, flashing, assembly, modification, or improper use.

Limited Editions

Limited-run, signed, serialized, or collectible items may have restricted return options.

Clearance / Final Sale

Future final-sale items may be ineligible for return unless defective or shipped incorrectly.

Return Workflow

Return Authorization Process

  1. 01Preserve Records:
    Keep order ID, invoice ID, transaction ID, product name, packaging, tracking, photos, and delivery records.
  2. 02Contact Support:
    Report the issue through the official Contact page before shipping anything back.
  3. 03Review Request:
    ZZX-Labs reviews product type, payment status, condition, delivery record, and issue category.
  4. 04Return Authorization:
    If approved, a return authorization, instructions, or replacement path may be issued.
  5. 05Ship Return:
    Customer ships the approved item according to instructions, packaging requirements, and tracking rules.
  6. 06Inspection:
    Returned item is inspected for condition, completeness, serial match, damage, use, modification, and missing parts.
  7. 07Resolution:
    Resolution may include replacement, repair, partial refund, refund, store credit, rejection, or support escalation.

Bitcoin Refunds

Refunds Are Separate From Returns

Returning a physical item does not automatically mean a Bitcoin refund is owed, approved, or immediately issued. Refunds depend on product type, condition, payment status, return approval, transaction records, shipping condition, and policy review.

Bitcoin transactions are generally irreversible at the protocol level. Refunds are therefore handled as a separate outgoing transaction after review, not as a reversal of the original payment.

Condition

Return Condition Requirements

Complete Contents

Return should include all parts, accessories, labels, manuals, inserts, cables, packaging, and kit components.

Original Condition

Items should be unused or minimally handled unless the return is for a verified defect or shipping damage.

No Unauthorized Modification

Modified, flashed, soldered, opened, damaged, or altered electronics may be rejected or partially refunded.

Photos Required

Photos may be required for damaged shipments, defective items, missing components, or wrong-item claims.

Shipping Damage

Damaged Packages

For damaged shipments, preserve the box, internal packaging, labels, inserts, product, and all visible damage. Take clear photos before discarding anything.

Carrier claims may require packaging evidence. Destroying the packaging too early may make a shipping claim difficult or impossible.

Exchanges

Replacements and Exchanges

When appropriate, ZZX-Labs may offer a replacement, exchange, repair, missing-part shipment, partial refund, store credit, or alternate resolution instead of a full refund.

Exchanges may depend on inventory, product availability, condition, original order records, and whether the product is a limited edition, discontinued item, external brand item, or custom kit.

External Brands

Stocked Vendor Products

Products made by external brands may also be subject to manufacturer warranties, distributor rules, packaging requirements, serial-number verification, or return limitations. ZZX-Labs does not control the policies of external manufacturers.

Support Checklist

What To Include In A Return Request

Order Details

Order ID, invoice ID, transaction ID, product name, quantity, and purchase date.

Issue Description

Explain whether it is damaged, defective, missing parts, wrong item, late shipment, or other issue.

Photos

Include photos of product, packaging, label, damage, serial number, and missing/incorrect contents.

Requested Resolution

State whether you request replacement, missing part, exchange, repair, refund, or support guidance.

Never send seed phrases, private keys, wallet files, passwords, recovery words, or sensitive credentials in a support request.
