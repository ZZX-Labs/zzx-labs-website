Rules

Only Original, Free, Open, or Authorized Music

The music catalog should avoid unauthorized commercial uploads. Every release should have clear ownership, license status, attribution requirements, redistribution terms, and takedown handling.

Original

Music made by ZZX-Labs, affiliated artists, collaborators, or commissioned projects.

Creative Commons

Music with clear CC license terms, attribution, source links, and derivative-use rules.

Public Domain

Only where the public-domain status is credible, documented, and jurisdictionally appropriate.

Authorized Mixes

DJ sets and mixtapes must use original, CC, public-domain, licensed, or otherwise authorized material.

No Unauthorized Uploads

No commercial tracks, label releases, ripped albums, or copyrighted third-party music without permission.

Takedown Ready

Any rights dispute should have a clear contact route and rapid review workflow.
