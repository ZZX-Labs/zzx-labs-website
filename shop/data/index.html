Data

The ZZX-Labs Data catalog contains structured records, JSON registries, CSV exports, SQLite databases, analytics files, Bitcoin datasets, market data, node data, mempool data, research data, metadata libraries, product records, publication indexes, and machine-readable technical resources.

Data products may be free, paid, open, licensed, subscriber-only, partner-only, bundled with reports, bundled with software, or delivered as custom research, analytics, API, or consulting work.

Privacy

Data Privacy and Safety

Data products should be designed with attention to lawful collection, privacy, minimization, source provenance, consent where required, security controls, retention limits, and responsible access.

Minimization

Collect and publish only what is necessary for the stated purpose.

Aggregation

Use aggregate records where individual-level records are not needed.

Retention

Define retention windows, archival rules, deletion rules, and update schedules.

Access Control

Restrict private, sensitive, licensed, paid, partner, or enterprise datasets.

Legal Review

Review privacy, compliance, terms, data source permissions, and jurisdictional limits.

Responsible Use

Avoid harmful uses, misrepresentation, unlawful surveillance, or unsafe publication of sensitive data.
