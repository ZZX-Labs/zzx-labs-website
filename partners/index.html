Bitcoin / India

BitTechIn

https://bit-tech.in

BitTechIn is a private Bitcoin mining technology consulting group and India-facing Bitcoin infrastructure concept connected to the broader ZZX-Labs incubation ecosystem.

Bitcoin Price Intelligence

bit-tick

https://bit-tick.in

bit-tick is a public open-source Bitcoin Price Index and website frontend using weighted price data from global exchanges to produce a fair weighted average quote. Its purpose is to compete with older Bitcoin price tools while providing a cleaner user experience without advertising or third-party link clutter.

Science / Bitcoin / Conservation

Speciedex

https://speciedex.org

Speciedex is a decentralized species cataloging concept using Bitcoin and Lightning Network security models to record, review, verify, and preserve data about Earth’s known species through distributed participation and parallel data structures.
