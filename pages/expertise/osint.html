Open Source Intelligence (OSINT)

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) is the process of gathering information from publicly available sources. At ZZX-Labs, we specialize in leveraging OSINT techniques to extract critical insights for our clients' strategic needs.

OSINT Services We Offer

  • Social Media Intelligence
  • Geospatial Intelligence (Geo-OSINT)
  • Dark Web Monitoring
  • Domain & Network Intelligence
  • Person & Entity Profiling
  • Threat Actor & Trend Tracking

We provide actionable insights by analyzing publicly available data from a wide range of sources, helping businesses and government agencies mitigate risks, make informed decisions, and detect emerging threats.
