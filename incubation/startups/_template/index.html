Copy Checklist

Before Publishing A Startup Page

  • Replace [Startup / Company Name] everywhere.
  • Replace the title and meta description.
  • Replace track, stage, status, posture, region, and funding fields.
  • Customize overview, mission, users, offering, and strategic role.
  • Remove the developer notice section from the public copy.
  • Add the startup card to incubation/startups/index.html.
  • Add related project, research, paper, download, and documentation links when available.
