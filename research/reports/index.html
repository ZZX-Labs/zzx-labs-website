Reports

The ZZX-Labs Reports library is the home for structured findings, technical assessments, security reviews, Bitcoin research reports, market reports, OSINT reports, infrastructure reviews, project status reports, benchmark reports, field notes, and formal investigative summaries.

Reports are more operational than papers. A paper explains a concept or system in formal research style. A report records findings, measurements, conclusions, recommendations, risks, evidence, status, or results. This page is designed to grow as ZZX-Labs releases new public, private, client, partner, and premium reports.

Lifecycle

Report Status Model

Planned

The report topic is identified, but collection, measurement, or writing has not started.

Collecting

Sources, logs, measurements, screenshots, datasets, observations, or evidence are being gathered.

Drafting

The report is being written, structured, formatted, reviewed, or converted into publishable form.

Internal

The report is private to ZZX-Labs, under internal review, or reserved for operational use.

Client / Partner

The report is restricted to a client, partner, institution, member, or subscription audience.

Published

The report has a stable release, version, publication date, canonical URL, download link, and verification records.

Updated

The report has been revised, amended, corrected, superseded, or expanded after publication.

Archived

The report is preserved for historical reference but may be outdated, replaced, or retired.

Report Metadata

Recommended Report Record Fields

Identity

Title, slug, report type, subtitle, executive summary, author, contributor, organization, and contact channel.

Classification

Domain, tags, access level, audience, status, version, priority, confidence, and related project.

Scope

Purpose, research question, included systems, excluded systems, assumptions, constraints, and limitations.

Evidence

Sources, datasets, screenshots, logs, measurements, references, citations, and supporting files.

Findings

Key findings, observations, impact, severity, confidence, recommendations, next steps, and unresolved questions.

Verification

PDF link, HTML link, manifest, SHA256 hash, GPG signature, canonical URL, and release notes.

Formats

Report Distribution Formats

HTML

Readable web version for public access, internal review, research portals, and citation linking.

PDF

Formal report format for clients, institutions, printing, archiving, signatures, and distribution.

Markdown

Source-friendly report format for repository storage, drafting, version control, and automation.

DOCX / ODT

Editable report formats for review, institutional delivery, custom client versions, and redlining.

CSV / XLSX

Supporting tables, evidence matrices, measurements, source logs, timeline sheets, and quantitative data.

Archive Bundle

ZIP, TAR, 7Z, report source files, appendices, evidence folders, hashes, screenshots, and manifests.
