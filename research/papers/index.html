This page is designed to grow over time as new ZZX-Labs papers are drafted, reviewed, published, revised, archived, and released as public or premium research materials.

Publication Status

Paper Lifecycle

Planned

Paper concept exists, but drafting has not begun or is not yet publicly visible.

Drafting

Paper is being written, structured, outlined, or assembled from notes and research material.

Internal Review

Paper is being reviewed for clarity, evidence, citations, risk, technical accuracy, and publication readiness.

Public Draft

Paper may be visible as a preliminary draft, subject to correction, revision, or expansion.

Published

Paper has a stable release version, citation data, download file, hash record, and publication date where available.

Archived

Paper is superseded, deprecated, retired, preserved for history, or replaced by a newer version.

Paper Metadata

Recommended Paper Record Fields

Every paper should be easy to cite, verify, download, archive, and revise. ZZX-Labs paper records should eventually follow a consistent metadata model.

Identity

Title, slug, subtitle, abstract, authors, contributors, organization, and contact channel.

Classification

Paper type, domain, tags, status, version, release stage, access level, and related project.

Dates

Date drafted, date reviewed, date published, date revised, date archived, and supersession notes.

Files

PDF, HTML, Markdown, source files, diagrams, appendices, datasets, references, and supporting downloads.

Verification

SHA256 hashes, signatures, manifest file, citation record, release notes, and canonical URL.

Licensing

Public, private, commercial, Creative Commons, MIT, internal, premium, or custom licensing terms.

Archive Bundle
