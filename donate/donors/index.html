Recognition Policy

Opt-In Recognition

ZZX-Labs does not automatically publish donor names.

Public acknowledgement requires explicit approval from the donor and may include:

  • Personal name
  • Organization name
  • Project sponsorship
  • Research sponsorship
  • Infrastructure sponsorship
  • Grant support
  • Anonymous recognition title
  • Public statement or message

Recognition may be modified or removed upon request.

Anonymous Donors

Donors who wish to remain private may do so indefinitely. Anonymous support is respected and encouraged where privacy is desired.

Named Donors

Donors who wish to be publicly recognized may appear within future donor registries, annual reports, acknowledgements, or project sponsorship records.

Research Sponsors

Support directed toward specific papers, reports, analyses, datasets, investigations, or publication initiatives.

Infrastructure Sponsors

Support directed toward hosting, servers, development systems, testing environments, mirrors, APIs, and long-term operational infrastructure.

Project Sponsors

Funding directed toward specific software, hardware, firmware, Bitcoin, cybersecurity, AI, ML, or research initiatives.

Grant Providers

Organizations, foundations, and institutions providing formal grants, fellowships, sponsorships, or strategic support.

Transparency

Independence and Integrity

Financial support does not automatically imply editorial control, research influence, publication authority, project ownership, or governance rights.

ZZX-Labs maintains the right to decline donations, sponsorships, partnerships, grants, or recognition requests that conflict with laboratory objectives, operational security, legal requirements, or ethical standards.
