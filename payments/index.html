Bitcoin Payments

ZZX-Labs R&D operates on a Bitcoin-first financial model. Payments for consulting, contracting, research, services, retainers, subscriptions, sponsorships, startup funding, shop purchases, API access, and donations are intended to settle through Bitcoin-native rails.

This page is the root gateway for all payment information across the site. It explains what may require payment, how payment categories are organized, how addresses and invoices should be verified, and which payment systems are planned or active.

Addresses and Invoices

Do Not Treat The Root Page As A Final Invoice

This payment hub should not be treated as a final invoice for large payments, consulting work, contracting, investments, grants, startup funding, or service retainers. Formal payments should use a specific invoice, quote, agreement, address record, or signed payment instruction.

A single static address may be useful for simple donations, but operational payment categories should eventually use unique addresses, invoices, labels, or Lightning invoices per transaction.

Payment Security

Verify Before Sending Bitcoin

Verify Address Text

Compare the full address text, not just the first and last characters.

Verify QR Codes

Confirm the QR code decodes to the exact expected address or invoice.

Verify Official URL

Use official ZZX-Labs pages and avoid copied screenshots, forwarded messages, or unknown mirrors.

Verify Amount

Confirm the invoice amount, fee expectations, due date, and payment purpose.

Verify Signatures

For high-value payments, request signed payment instructions and compare against key history.

Stop If Suspicious

If anything looks wrong, do not send funds. Contact ZZX-Labs through official channels.

Infrastructure

Planned Payment Architecture

ZZX-Labs payment systems should eventually be backed by Bitcoin-native infrastructure rather than custodial payment processors. The target architecture may include full nodes, Lightning nodes, BTCPay Server, LND, watch-only accounting, multisig treasury storage, signed invoices, cold storage, and internal payment APIs.

Bitcoin Core

Full node infrastructure for verification, address monitoring, wallet policy, and network sovereignty.

Lightning Node

LND or compatible Lightning infrastructure for fast low-value and recurring payments.

BTCPay Server

Self-hosted payment server for invoices, shop payments, service billing, and merchant workflows.

Multisig Treasury

Future cold-storage and multisignature controls for larger balances and organizational funds.

Watch-Only Accounting

Auditable address monitoring without exposing private keys to web infrastructure.

Signed Payment Instructions

High-value payments should support GPG-signed address and invoice verification.

Wallets

Wallet Compatibility

ZZX-Labs does not require a specific wallet for ordinary Bitcoin payments. Users are responsible for choosing secure wallets, verifying payment details, backing up keys, and protecting funds.

Bitcoin Core

Full-node wallet and reference implementation for sovereign users.

Sparrow

Advanced desktop wallet for hardware-wallet, multisig, and coin-control workflows.

Electrum

Lightweight Bitcoin wallet with advanced transaction and signing features.

Hardware Wallets

Recommended for larger balances and long-term key security.

Zeus / Phoenix / Breez

Lightning-capable wallets for future low-fee L2 payment flows.

Watch-Only Tools

Useful for confirming payment receipt without exposing signing keys.
