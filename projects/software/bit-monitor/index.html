Overview

A lean Bitcoin node monitor focused on actionable signals. Pulls data from your own full node(s) and optional public relays to visualize mempool pressure, feerates, orphan/stale rates, policy edges (standardness, relay), peer churn, IBS/IBD status, bandwidth, and chain reorg hints. Ships with rule-based alerts (webhook/email) and CLI/JSON output so you can wire it into ops.
