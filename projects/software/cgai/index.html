Overview

CGAI is an opt-in, adults-only conversational and creative assistant. It supports text, image, and audio generation (with TTS/STT) and optional video composition workflows. The system enforces strict safety rails: verified 18+ access, consent prompts, granular content controls, red-team filters, refusal logic, watermarking, hashed content provenance, and configurable regional compliance modes. Local-first profiles, encryption at rest, and clear revocation paths are core to the design.
