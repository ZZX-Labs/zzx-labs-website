Overview

SynthLavaRNG is a digital reimagining of SGI’s 1996 Lavarand and Noll/Cooper’s LavaRnd concepts — an offline, GUI-driven entropy engine that harnesses simulated visual chaos. Each frame from animated “lava lamp” presets (metaballs, fireflies, oil projectors, magnetic fields, and more) is hashed with SHA3-256 to maintain a continuously evolving entropy pool. An HMAC-DRBG (SHA3-256) is reseeded periodically or manually, ensuring unbiased, high-quality random output for local applications, cryptographic testing, and secure seeding workflows.
