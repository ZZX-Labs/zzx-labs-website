Overview

Cryptainer provides sealed, verifiable containers for private material and datasets. Built for self-custody and secure archival, Cryptainer supports layerable encryption, policy manifests, integrity hashes, and timelock conditions. Use it for wallets, seedphrase archives, signed backups, or reproducible sealed data bundles that can be released or audited without exposing secrets.

        Highlights

        • Layered encryption with pluggable cipher suites and optional hardware-backed keys.
        • Policy manifests describing access rules, acceptable exposure, and expiration/timelock conditions.
        • SHA-256/Blake2b integrity hashes and signed manifests for auditability.
        • Timelock release (nLockTime-style or scripted time-based unlocks) for escrowed releases.
        • Export/import as signed, versioned bundles; streaming-friendly for large datasets.
        • Offline-first design; key derivation and passphrase hardening tuned for usability and security.
