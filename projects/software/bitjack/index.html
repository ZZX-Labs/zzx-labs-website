Overview

BitJack is a Bitcoin-native Blackjack engine. Rounds use verifiable randomness with commit–reveal or VRF seeding, while Lightning enables instant micro-bets and payouts. The table flow is deterministic and replayable for audits: deck shuffles, player actions, and dealer hits are derived from public seeds and hashed transcripts. Operators can run single-table or multi-table lobbies with sane house edges and watch-only accounting.
