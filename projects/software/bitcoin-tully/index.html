Overview

Bitcoin Tully builds an annotated timeline (“tally”) of UTXOs to study coin age, dormancy, realized-cap contributions, and flow paths — without invasive surveillance. It normalizes node/electrum/esplora data, computes lifecycle metrics, and exports clean tables for research & dashboards.
