XConStats

Wireless Xbox controller status & readout GUI for PC. Live buttons/axes, dead-zone tuning, rumble tests, and multi-pad view — all in your browser.

Live Controller Readout

Connect an Xbox controller via Bluetooth or dongle. Press a button to wake it. Then click Start to begin polling with the Gamepad API.

Real-time Readout

Buttons, triggers, sticks, and D-pad updates at your chosen rate using the Gamepad API.

Dead-Zone Tuning

Clamp micro-jitter around center for cleaner visualizations and logging.

Multi-Pad

Monitor multiple connected controllers simultaneously — great for QA or tournaments.

Rumble Check

Optional vibration test when haptics are supported by your browser & device.

OBS Friendly

Compact UI blocks & dark theme that drop into streaming overlays and recordings.

Privacy-Respecting

No network calls required for the demo. Data stays in your browser unless you export.

Enable Gamepad

  1. Pair your Xbox controller to the OS.
  2. Tap any button to wake it.
  3. Click Start above.
