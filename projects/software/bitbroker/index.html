Overview

BitBroker is a venue-agnostic router for executing Bitcoin orders across centralized exchanges and liquidity endpoints, with a research-grade toolbox for slippage, queue position, and impact analysis. It normalizes market data, simulates routing policies, and ships with adapters you can extend.
