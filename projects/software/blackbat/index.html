Overview

BlackBat is a suite of algorithmic strategies designed to increase bitcoin holdings through disciplined, research-driven trade rules. Focuses include volatility harvesting, time-weighted accumulation, liquidity-aware limit routing, and hedged position sizing with risk controls suitable for custodial or non-custodial execution.
