Overview

BitContractor is a Bitcoin-first workflow for hiring and delivery. Define milestones with testable acceptance criteria, fund non-custodial escrows via PSBT multisig, and release payouts based on attestations and verifiable artifacts. Optional DLC/Oracle hooks let you encode objective outcomes (e.g., “mainnet service up 7 days”). Every step leaves a clear, exportable audit trail — specs, commits, hashes, signatures, and transactions.
