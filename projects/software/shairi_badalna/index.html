What is Shairi Badalna?

Shairi Badalna organizes large video libraries by content & structure. It fingerprints files (hash), extracts technical metadata, lets you add tags/collections, previews target paths with dry-run safety, and can export plans (CSV/JSON) for CLI automation. Use it standalone or embed as a library.
