Overview

GGAI is the curated gallery companion for CGAI. It handles creator onboarding, content ingestion, moderation queues, rights & licensing, watermarking, and distribution. It integrates safety filters, age/consent checks, and comprehensive audit logs. Optional Lightning payouts, token-gated collections, and federated mirrors can be enabled.

        Highlights

        • Vetted creators: KYC-style verification (configurable), consent attestations, and contract-backed uploads.
        • Moderation: Multi-stage review, policy presets, redaction tools, and appeal workflow.
        • Safety: On-device filters, opt-in blocklists, geo/age gating, and traceable publishing with audit logs.
        • Provenance: Perceptual hashing, metadata manifests, signing, and duplicate detection across mirrors.
        • Licensing: Rights templates (personal, commercial, editorial), auto-watermarking & license tokens.
        • Commerce: Tip jars, subscriptions, bundles; optional Lightning/Bitcoin payout rails.
        • Integrations: First-class CGAI pipeline hooks, CDN/edge caching, and federated gallery sync.
