ZZX-MSP

Managed Service Platform · Multi-tenant ops console for monitoring, backups, patching, access, billing, incidents, and runbooks — with zero-trust per-client isolation.

What ZZX-MSP Covers

Multi-Tenant Isolation

Separate org spaces, RBAC, per-tenant secrets, scoped logs, policy as code, and audit trails.

Monitoring & Alerts

Fleet health, SLOs/SLA targets, alert routing and on-call schedules; ready Grafana boards.

Backups & Patching

Scheduled snapshots, integrity checks, restore drills; OS/package patch sets with windows.

Access & Zero-Trust

Just-in-time credentials, short-lived tunnels, hardware-key MFA, per-action approval gates.

Runbooks & Automations

Composable steps (Ansible/Terraform/CLI). Dry-run first, change sets reviewed then applied.

Billing + Tickets

Usage metering, invoices, and integrations for tickets & incidents (Jira, GitHub, Linear).
