Overview

HashPig is a speedy, user-friendly hasher for desktops and servers. Point it at a folder (or drop files), and it computes BLAKE3/SHA-256/SHA-512/xxHash in parallel, with optional Merkle roots and signed manifests. Great for release packaging, artifact verification, CI pipelines, and quick integrity spot-checks.

        Highlights

        • Drag-and-drop UI: hash files/folders instantly; copy results or export manifests.
        • Parallel engine: multi-threaded BLAKE3/SHA variants with adaptive chunking.
        • Manifests: JSONL/CSV/SRI outputs, optional signature & timestamp.
        • Verify mode: re-scan and diff against prior manifests; clear pass/fail reports.
        • CLI & library: integrate with build/release pipelines and CI runners.
        • Cross-platform: Linux, macOS, Windows; portable binary option.
