Overview

bitcoin-mined is a lightweight analytics layer for miners and pool participants. It ingests recent blocks, extracts subsidy/fees, MEV-like outliers (large fee spikes), pool tags, and coinbase commitments, then correlates them with your payout receipts. The goal: transparent accounting, easier tax/export, and fast detection of pool-side anomalies.
