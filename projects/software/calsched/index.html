Overview

CalSched is a scheduling toolkit that integrates with CalDAV/ICS sources and major calendars. It offers availability windows, time-zone aware booking links, pooled resources (e.g., round-robin teams), and guardrails like buffer times, daily caps, and lead times. Designed to be self-hostable, auditably secure, and API-first for embedding in apps or portals.
