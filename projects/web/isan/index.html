ISAN

ISAN (Internet Streaming Audio Network) is a sovereign streaming platform designed to deliver encrypted, real-time audio channels globally. It acts as a distributed radio network, supporting music, comms, podcasts, and secure staff voice feeds.

The system is fully integrated with GPG encryption, ensuring confidentiality and integrity of every audio stream. It can operate as a standalone node or as part of the larger ISRN/ISVN network stack.

Key Features

  • Encrypted audio streaming (radio-like global network)
  • Supports live broadcasts and on-demand playback
  • Compatible with ISRN relay tunnels
  • Low-latency peer-to-peer streaming model
  • Staff and public channels with access controls
