bit-tech.in

BitTechIn is our Asian Bitcoin Research & Development Lab — operating in parallel to the American counterpart BitTech. The lab focuses exclusively on Bitcoin L1 and L2 technologies, spanning Web, Software, Firmware, Hardware, Mining, and Security design & development.

BitTechIn also administers the full Bit-Tick stack — including the API, hardware tickers, and web tickers — ensuring reliable market telemetry for research programs, operations, and field deployments.

Focus Areas

  • Bitcoin protocol R&D (L1) and Lightning Network (L2)
  • Web portals, dashboards, and operator consoles
  • Software services, daemons, and data pipelines
  • Firmware for embedded devices & hardware tickers
  • Hardware design for mining & telemetry appliances
  • Security architecture, key mgmt, and hardening

Bit-Tick Administration

  • API stewardship (ingestion, aggregation, availability)
  • Hardware ticker firmware & enclosure reference designs
  • Web tickers and embeddable widgets for portals
  • Metrics: latency, drift, precision, failover behavior
