Operating Standard

How To Approach ZZX-Labs

Clear proposals move faster. Include who you are, what you want to build or support, why ZZX-Labs is relevant, what resources you bring, what timeline you imagine, whether the work is public or private, and whether secure communication is required.

Sensitive cybersecurity, infrastructure, research, legal, or partnership discussions should use GPG-secured communication when appropriate.
