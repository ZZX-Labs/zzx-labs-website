Operating Principle

Pseudonymity, Privacy, and Operational Security

Staff pages should not expose unnecessary personal information. For sensitive research, cybersecurity, infrastructure, Bitcoin, defense-adjacent work, or private consulting activity, pseudonymous profiles and stylized placeholder identities are preferred until public attribution is required.

Each staff member may eventually receive a dedicated subpage under staff/, similar to the consultant, partner, and startup profile systems. Profiles can include role, domain, public work, projects, publications, skills, contact routing, and disclosure level.
