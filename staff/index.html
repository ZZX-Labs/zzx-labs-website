Our Staff, Operators, Researchers & Collaborators

ZZX-Labs R&D operates as a pseudonymous, privacy-preserving, research-driven technology laboratory supported by a developing network of researchers, engineers, analysts, developers, designers, Bitcoin operators, security specialists, infrastructure builders, advisors, consultants, collaborators, and strategic allies.

Public staff records may use handles, codenames, role profiles, or placeholder identities. Sensitive work involving Bitcoin infrastructure, cybersecurity, OSINT, private R&D, defense-adjacent research, infrastructure operations, or strategic advisory activity may remain pseudonymous, private, restricted, or redacted.

Operating Principle

Pseudonymity, Privacy, and Operational Security

This directory is designed to show organizational capability without exposing unnecessary personal information. ZZX-Labs can publish role-based public profiles for staff, advisors, fellows, operators, and collaborators while preserving personal safety, operational security, and privacy.

Avatar art is intended to use a consistent black balaclava and hoodie motif, with each figure uniquely differentiated by posture, lighting, accent color, patch, division marker, and expression. These avatars are stylized identity shields, not claims of criminality, militancy, or unlawful activity.

Disclosure Levels

Profile Visibility Model

Public

Full public profile approved for attribution, project credits, publications, and contact routing.

Pseudonymous

Role and work may be listed under a handle or codename without personal identification.

Private

Internal staff or collaborator known to ZZX-Labs but not publicly listed beyond a placeholder role.

Restricted

Profile omitted or heavily redacted due to operational security, legal, contractual, or safety needs.
