Progress Summary

Current Development State

The site has moved from a collection of minimal standalone pages toward a structured, reusable system with shared design patterns, page shells, card grids, templates, JSON-backed workflows, and future generated directories.

The largest completed improvement so far is the unification of top-level page style and the creation of repeatable page families for incubation, opportunities, staff, partners, notice tracking, and startup profiles.
