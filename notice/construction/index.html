Purpose

Why Construction Notices Exist

ZZX-Labs is being built as a large interconnected ecosystem consisting of research archives, project portfolios, startup incubation systems, service offerings, Bitcoin infrastructure, software repositories, documentation portals, and future secure services.

Construction notices allow visitors to understand which areas are incomplete while preserving site structure, navigation, and future expansion paths.

Construction Standards

Completion Criteria

A page is generally considered complete when:

  • Layout matches the unified design system.
  • Navigation paths are finalized.
  • Documentation is present.
  • Links resolve correctly.
  • Mobile layouts are tested.
  • Assets are optimized.
  • Accessibility issues are addressed.
  • Placeholder content is removed.
  • Data sources are verified.
  • Future maintenance workflows exist.
