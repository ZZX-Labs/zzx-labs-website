Our History

ZZX-Labs was founded on the principle of harnessing cutting-edge technology, openly, to solve real-world problems. Over the years, we have grown from a small team of innovators to a globally recognized leader in private research and development.

Milestones

  • 2008 - ZZX-Labs was founded with a focus on technology & cybersecurity research.
  • 2009 - Bitcoin goes live, ZZX-Labs begins researching bitcoin's potential.
  • 2010 - ZZX-Labs begins mining bitcoin in San Francisco.
  • 2011 - ZZX-Labs expands into web development using bitcoin payment integration.
  • 2012 - 0xdeadbeef begins professionally consulting in the field of bitcoin technology.
  • 2013 - ZZX-Labs begins working on bitcoin algorithmic trading systems.
  • 2014 - ZZX-Labs starts to begin working on 3rd layer bitcoin network technology for smart contract support on bitcoin's network.
  • 2015 - Agrees to work towards the release of the Speciedex blockchain, based on bitcoin's technology & design for the purpose of cataloging, recording, & document all of Earth's species remaining.
  • 2016 - Segways into data type metatagging for file type contents of media data.
  • 2017 - Expanded into artificial intelligence and machine learning innovations.
  • 2018 - Begins development of private offline trained GPT PDAs.
  • 2019 - Developed industry-leading hardware prototypes for custom bitcoin standalone hardware & controller handsets.
  • 2020 - Created over 120 different cryptographic hashing, crc, encryption ciphers, & encryption algorithms as well as randomness & entropy seed RNG algorithms for new types of cryptographic applications.
  • 2021 - Created several suites of open-source tools for developers and researchers.
  • 2022 - Created numerous software designs & concepts for real world solutions to current issues.
  • 2023 - Established private research projects in technology, cyber security, & defense sectors.
  • 2024 - Launched ZZX-Labs website.
  • 2025 - Expanding into bitcoin forensics, analytics, tracking, reporting, & investigation services. – TBA.

Looking Ahead

As we continue to grow, ZZX-Labs remains dedicated to advancing technology responsibly, creating impactful solutions, and shaping the future of innovation.
