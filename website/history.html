Our History

ZZX-Labs was founded on the principle of harnessing cutting-edge technology to solve real-world problems. Over the years, we have grown from a small team of innovators to a globally recognized leader in private research and development.

Milestones

  • 2015 - ZZX-Labs was founded with a focus on cybersecurity research.
  • 2017 - Expanded into artificial intelligence and machine learning innovations.
  • 2019 - Developed industry-leading hardware prototypes for secure computing.
  • 2021 - Launched a suite of open-source tools for developers and researchers.
  • 2023 - Established partnerships with global leaders in technology and defense sectors.

Looking Ahead

As we continue to grow, ZZX-Labs remains dedicated to advancing technology responsibly, creating impactful solutions, and shaping the future of innovation.
