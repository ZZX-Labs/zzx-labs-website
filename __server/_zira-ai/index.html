ZIRA Policy

Principles for responsible deployment.

Human Oversight

ZIRA assists operations but should not replace human review for security, legal, financial, or research decisions.

Zero Trust

Access should be segmented, authenticated, logged, and auditable.

Research First

Prioritize documentation, reproducibility, evidence, and transparent methodology.

Protect Data

Never expose confidential datasets, private prompts, model weights, credentials, or customer information.
