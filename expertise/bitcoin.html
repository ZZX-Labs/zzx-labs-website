Bitcoin and Lightning Network Technologies

Bitcoin and Lightning Network technology have revolutionized the world of finance, security, and decentralized computing. At ZZX-Labs, we specialize in researching and advancing these technologies.

Our Bitcoin Research Areas

Our research covers several crucial aspects of Bitcoin and its underlying blockchain technology, including:

  • Bitcon 2nd and 3rd layer network architecture and security
  • Cryptographic protocols used in Bitcoin
  • Bitcoin protocol and consensus mechanisms
  • Smart contract functionality and decentralized finance (DeFi)
  • Bitcoin scalability solutions and optimization techniques

We also explore the future of Bitcoin, focusing on scaling solutions, energy efficiency, and global adoption.
